## 📖 Project Overview

**KeenKeeper** is a sophisticated friendship management dashboard designed to help users nurture and maintain meaningful connections. It ensures that no friend is left behind by tracking interaction history, setting relationship goals, and providing insightful analytics on your communication habits.

## 🌟 Key Features

### ### 📡 1. Automated Interaction & Global Sync
Every time you log a **Call, Text, or Video** check-in, the app utilizes the **Context API** to instantly sync data across the platform. Your interaction count and analytics update immediately without needing a page refresh.

### ### 🚥 2. Visual Relationship Health Tracking
The app features an intelligent status system: **Overdue, Almost Due, and On-Track**. Friend cards automatically change their visual styling and background colors based on these statuses, helping you identify which relationships need attention at a glance.

### ### 📈 3. Dynamic Analytics & Timeline Filtering
KeenKeeper offers a dedicated **Stats Page** with **Recharts** integration for data visualization. Additionally, the **Timeline Page** includes a robust filtering system, allowing you to sort history by specific interaction types.
